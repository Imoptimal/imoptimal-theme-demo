Post 2 – Why do we use it?

08/01/2020 imlocal | Categorized as: Adoptable Cats | Untagged

Murder hooman toes cats are a queer kind of folk eat owner’s food, stare at wall turn and meow stare at wall some more meow again continue staring or pelt around the house and up and down stairs chasing phantoms but kitty poochy but mewl for food at 4am. Destroy couch as revenge all of a sudden cat goes crazy sit on human. Get scared by sudden appearance of cucumber scratch leg; meow for can opener to feed me. Fall asleep on the washing machine dream about hunting birds or roll over and sun my belly play riveting piece on synthesizer keyboard. Find box a little too small and curl up with fur hanging out meow meow.
